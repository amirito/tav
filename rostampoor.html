Mr. Mohammad Rostampoor

He was born in 1964 in East Azarbayejan of Iran.

He received his B.S in civil engineering and then he received occupation

license grade 1 in design calculation and performance from ministry of urban and development affaires and housing.

He has worked in designing, supervising, implementing official,commercial and residential complexes and hotel buildings.

He has established an investment company for construction and selling buildings.

Mr. Rostampoor has established Tav group holding which is made up different companies.

Tav group has worked in constructional, industrial, petrochemical and cultural activities.

Mr. Rostampoor's participating in social activities guarantees the progression and development, so he is one of the pragmatists in cultural, social and welfare activities.
